Everything You Need

Powerful features designed for field operations

Project Management

Organize multiple initiatives. Assign sites and workers to specific projects like "Disaster Relief" or "Education Drive".

Geo-Fenced Attendance

Ensure accountability. Workers can only clock in when physically present at the assigned site location.

Real-Time Reporting

Collect data instantly. Volunteers submit photo reports and task updates directly from their mobile.

Leave Management

Handle time-off requests seamlessly. Workers request leave, admins approve with one click.

Team Messaging

Stay connected. Send broadcasts to all staff or chat directly with individual team members.

Data Export

Export attendance logs, reports, and analytics as CSV or JSON for your records and reporting needs.
